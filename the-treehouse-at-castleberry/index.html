What is The Treehouse?

The Treehouse is a platform for modern, modular destinations—where local food, retail, and events come together in architected container parks. We obsess over the brand and experience first, then bring it to life with flexible structures and vibrant programming.

The Treehouse at Castleberry

Located minutes from downtown Atlanta, Castleberry Hill is an arts-forward district with deep community roots. Our park blends curated food, retail, and event spaces with a lush, playful atmosphere.

  • Modular containers with flexible unit sizes
  • Anchored by local F&B and rotating pop-ups
  • Public green and event stage for weekly programming
  • Designed for quick deployment and long-term value
