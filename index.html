What is The Treehouse?

The Treehouse is a platform for modern, modular destinations—where local food, retail, and events come together in architected container parks. We obsess over the brand and experience first, then bring it to life with flexible structures and vibrant programming for families.

Why Shipping Containers?

Every year, thousands of shipping containers are left unused in ports around the world. Instead of becoming waste, repurposing them for commercial and community spaces is an innovative way to give them a second life.

This reuse helps reduce industrial waste, lowers the carbon footprint, and promotes a circular economy. In this way, we turn an environmental problem into an opportunity to create unique, sustainable, and modern places.

Respecting Nature & Ecosystems

Our project is designed to minimize impact on the land. We avoid disturbing the soil and strive to preserve as many existing trees as possible, allowing rainwater and runoff to flow naturally beneath the site.

By maintaining the native trees and protecting the local fauna, we help sustain the ecosystem and create a harmonious environment for both people and wildlife. Our approach ensures that nature remains an integral part of The Treehouse experience.

The Treehouse at Castleberry

Located minutes from downtown Cumming, Castleberry Road is a thoroughfare where the fairgrounds and the town center are a traditional meeting point. Our park combines a curated selection of restaurants, amenities, and event spaces with a family-friendly and fun atmosphere.

Available Container Units: We offer 1 double container (40' + 20') perfect for an anchor restaurant, 3 standard 40' containers ideal for food restaurants, and 4 compact 20' containers designed for beverages, smoothies, coffee, and desserts.

  • Modular containers with flexible unit sizes
  • Anchored by local F&B and rotating pop-ups
  • Public green and event stage for weekly programming
  • Designed for quick deployment and long-term value
